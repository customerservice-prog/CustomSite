FAQ

What areas do you serve?

Onondaga County and most of CNY. Delivery radius for free drop-off is 30 miles from Syracuse; farther by quote.

How far in advance should I book?

Weekends in summer fill fast—2–4 weeks ahead for large tents. Short notice possible based on inventory.

Do you set up and tear down?

Yes. Setup and strike are included in your quote for standard packages.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Contact us 7+ days before for full credit toward a new date; inside 7 days, a restock fee may apply per contract.

Are your inflatables safe and inspected?

All units are cleaned, insured, and inspected per manufacturer and state guidelines.
