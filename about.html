Who you’re hiring

CustomSite is me (Bryan) and a small group of people I bring in when a project needs it — not a call center, not a “we’ll get back to you” ticket queue. I’m based in Syracuse, NY and I work with local and national businesses; Central New York clients get priority for in-person and quick visits when that helps.

You get the same person from first call to launch — and someone you can reach when something breaks. Book a free 20-minute call or send a message and tell me what’s not working on your current site.
