Who we are

CustomSite is a web design and development agency in Syracuse, NY. We’re a tight team of strategists, designers, and developers who work with small and mid-sized businesses. Every project gets a dedicated lead from discovery through launch — and we stay available after go-live. We serve Central New York and clients nationwide. Local clients get priority for in-person kickoffs when that helps; most of our work runs over video and shared docs just as well.
