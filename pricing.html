Why not just use Fiverr?

Fiverr is fine for a $99 logo or a quick template swap. A site that ranks in local search, loads in under two seconds on a phone, and has a contact path that actually works is a different job — it takes scoping, revisions, and someone who still answers the phone after launch.

With us you get a fixed written scope, direct access to the builder (Bryan, Syracuse, NY), and optional care if you do not want to babysit hosting and updates. We are not the cheapest; we are the option when “cheap” already cost you a season of bad leads.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most sites are completed within 1-3 weeks depending on complexity. E-commerce sites may take 3-5 weeks. We will give you an exact timeline during our initial call.

Do I own my website?

Yes, 100%. Once you pay the build fee, the website is yours. If you ever cancel maintenance, you can take the files and host it anywhere you want.

Is the maintenance plan required?

No, it is optional. But we strongly recommend it for security, backups, and keeping your site running smoothly.

What if I need more pages or features?

No problem. We can add extra pages for $75-$150 each depending on complexity, or custom features starting at $200.

Do you build on WordPress?

Yes! We use WordPress, Shopify, or custom HTML/CSS depending on what is best for your needs. We will recommend the right platform.

What is your payment policy?

50% deposit to start, 50% on delivery. For larger projects we can do milestone-based payments.

Do you use templates or fully custom work?

We do not hand you a generic “theme site” and disappear. The final product is custom for your brand and content. Depending on the plan, we may use a battle-tested base on WordPress or Shopify so you can update content easily — the design, structure, and integrations are still tailored to you.

What level of SEO do you include?

Every build includes on-page SEO: titles, meta descriptions, clean headings, fast mobile performance, sitemap, and analytics hookup. Ongoing content SEO and local citation campaigns are available as add-on scope — we can quote after your goals and market.

What happens if I need changes after launch?

Each tier includes a set number of revision rounds before launch. After go-live, small fixes during the included support window are covered. Ongoing content and feature work is available through an optional care plan or hourly blocks — we always quote before you commit.

Can you take over a site someone else built?

Yes, in most cases. We will audit the stack, hosting, and what you have access to, then give you a fixed quote to stabilize, restyle, or rebuild — whichever is most cost-effective for your situation.

How do I know I will not be stuck with a vendor I cannot fire?

You own the work after the build is paid. You get credentials, exports where applicable, and clear documentation. If you cancel a care plan, you can take the project elsewhere — we are happy to do a handoff call.
