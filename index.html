Project timeline

How a build unfolds

Clear phases so you know what is happening and when. Timelines flex with store size and your review speed — we quote a calendar after discovery.

  1. Week 1

    Discovery & brief

    Kickoff call, goals, sitemap, brand assets, and technical constraints. You receive a written scope and success criteria.

  2. Weeks 2–3

    Structure & wire direction

    Page hierarchy, key modules, and mobile-first layout plan. E‑commerce adds catalog and checkout flow mapping.

  3. Weeks 3–5

    Design & build

    UI design in your brand, then development on the agreed platform. Integrations (forms, payments, analytics) are wired in this phase.

  4. Weeks 5–6

    Review rounds

    Staged link for your team. Revisions are tracked against your plan’s included rounds: copy tweaks, layout polish, and launch checklist.

  5. Go-live

    Launch & handoff

    DNS, SSL, redirects, and analytics verification. You get ownership credentials, a short Loom or doc walkthrough, and optional care plan if you want us on hook for hosting and updates.
