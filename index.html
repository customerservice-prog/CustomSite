Why most sites don’t convert

Your traffic is paying off someone else’s funnel until you fix the leaks

Industry studies and our audits consistently show 30–70% abandonment before a meaningful action — slow mobile, vague offers, buried CTAs, no conversion path. That isn’t “web design”; it’s customer acquisition failure you can measure.

  • Missed calls & form fills

    Visitors bounce when load times drag or the next step isn’t obvious. You don’t see those people again — they call the business that felt instant.

  • Weaker trust than you deserve

    If your site doesn’t match how good you are in person, buyers choose whoever looks established. Credibility is a conversion layer, not decoration.

  • Search & speed compound

    Google rewards fast, clear, mobile-first experiences. A dated stack doesn’t just annoy users — it costs visibility while others climb.
